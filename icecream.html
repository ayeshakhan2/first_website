Vegan Banana Ice Cream

Total Time : 3 hours

Ingredients

  • 5 bananas
  • 24 chocolate sandwich cookies
  • 4 cups peanut butter
  • 4 cups dark chocolate
  • 4 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Chop up some bananas and freeze in container for at least two hours.
  2. Blend until the bananas are smooth.
  3. Add some chocolate sandwich cookies, peanut butter, dark chocolate and chocolate chips and blend again.
  4. Pour into a container and freeze until solid.
  5. Enjoy!
