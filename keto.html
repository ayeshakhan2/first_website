Keto Chocolate Mousse

Total Time : 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 3 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup powdered zero-calorie sweetener (such as SwerveÂ®)
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder

Directions

  1. Place cream cheese in a large bowl and beat using an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Turn mixer to low speed and slowly add heavy cream and vanilla extract. Add sweetener, cocoa powder and salt, mixing until well incorporated. Turn mixer to high, and mix until light and fluffy, 1 to 2 minutes more. Serve immediately, or refrigerate for later.

Nutrition

  • Per Serving
    • 373 calories
    • 37.6 g fat
    • 6.9 g carbohydrates
    • 5.4 g protein
    • 128 mg cholesterol
    • 227 mg sodium.
